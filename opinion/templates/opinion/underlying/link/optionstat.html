Greek chart

  • IV term: Is current iv in short term spike (after big move) or long term stable.
  • IV skew: Skew iv happen when ATM iv is lower than ITM or OTM.
  • Theta skew: Usually ATM theta will be lowest. If not, it is skew.
  • Vega skew: Usually ATM vega will be lowest. If not, it is skew.
  • Greek charts: Use thinkorswim option deep to create image and save it for analysis.

Today options stats

  • Option stat iv: Basic options stats IV/HV for 52 weeks high/low.
  • Option sizzle: Compare call to put for today trading volume.
  • Option bid/ask: Today option traded bid/ask range for better enter/exit.

Special case

  • Open interest: Use current open interest as major bull/bear indicator because hedge fund only use them as protection. More calls holding is bear protection or covered; more puts holding is call protection.
