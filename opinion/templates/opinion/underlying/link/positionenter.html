Helper

Signal

  • Direction: side you want to trade.
  • Close: current close price.

Strategy

  • Primary strategy you want to trade. Stock or options. Vertical or skew butterfly.

Commission

  • Fee for enter and exit. IB stock $1, options $1; TDA stock $9, options $1.5.

P/L Profile

  • Risk profile for this trade. Capital, margin, profit/loss and expect return.

Date & Event

  • Expected enter and exit date. Sometime things go wrong, maybe you exit early or hold longer.
  • Do this trade cross event or it is a event trade?

Price chart

  • Full thinkorswim p/l chart analysis. Can also be technical chart or others.

Excel report

  • Excel report that used to keep track everyday move.
