Risk adjust

Main analysis

Update research

When ever your position is in danger, you need to rework all you research. Maybe the research you do is expire or no up to date. If you trade stock, re-do the stock report. If you trade etf, re-do the etf report. (Advantage: Large)

Report enter

Excel analysis

You trade stock or options strategy, you always have excel spreadsheet to keep track on everything. You should also make your decision based on excel report. (Advantage: Small)

Do backtest

You need to backtest current state of the market price to determine the next price movement. If you test enough, you should know the next price move favor your trade or not. How long recover, how much volume, on what situation. (Advantage: Medium)

Mindset

Clam your mind

Loss? You have a rough day. You need to clam your mind. Start with some "before market open" step. Take shower, listen music, take a sleep and eat something. After all this, your mind is back and you can make a better decision.

Understand risk

At the moment before you enter a trade, do you know the risk? If you know the risk then you can take it when it loss. If the loss is bigger than what you expect and you do not have a plan for that then is you fault. Try to understand the risk when you enter the trade, will help you make exit decision more easy.
