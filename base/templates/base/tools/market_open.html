Holding

The longest trading period is holding. Holding can be difficult; holding can be easy. If nothing happen, no news, no price move, no event, then mostly holding a positions is just let it run. But something thing got tricky. Special news, special announcement, sudden no news big price move, market swift changing and etc. All those will put your holding position in danger. So extra analysis is done when things happen. (Advantage: Medium)

Trading process

Report enter

Whatever you think you want to enter a trade, remember you need to do the enter report first. Stock enter report or ETF enter report. Doing enter report will let you re-evaluate the instrument you want to trade. From fundamental to technical, the enter report include everything that you can find in the market/web. You should also check past historical position & report you made. Sometime a symbol just not profit no matter how you trade, you should avoid them. (Advantage: Large)
